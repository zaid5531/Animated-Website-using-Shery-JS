Ray-Ban was founded in 1936 by Bausch & Lomb, an American company. The initial purpose of the brand was to create sunglasses for U.S. Army Air Corps pilots to reduce glare and protect their eyes during flight.

Ray-Ban has several iconic sunglasses models, including the Aviator, Wayfarer, and Clubmaster. Each of these models has its own unique style and has been popular for many decades.
